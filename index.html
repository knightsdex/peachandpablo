Web3 Community

Peach and Pablo is an emerging character IP built around two cats whose viral presence has generated a global, cross-platform audience. Their recognisable visual identity and consistent performance across short-form content form the foundation of a scalable multimedia franchise. We are developing Peach and Pablo into a full IP ecosystem spanning books, plush collectibles, animated shorts, YouTube formats and experiential activations. The Peach and Pablo token acts as the core digital asset within this ecosystem, enabling community participation as the franchise expands and the universe matures.
